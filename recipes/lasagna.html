Lasagna

Description

Making a lasagna is fairly simple. As a brief overview, we will first be making the meat sauce followed by cooking the noodles. We will then make the ricotta mixture, and go through a process to layer the lasagna. Aftewards, we will cover the foil and bake. Finally, make sure to let the lasagna rest for a brief period of time before serving it!

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian Sausage and Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion and Garlic:One Onion and Two cloves of Garlic
  • Tomato Products:One can of Crushed Tomatoes, Two cans of Tomato Sauce, and Two cans of Tomato Paste
  • Sugar:Two tablespoons of White Sugar
  • Spices/Seasonings:Fresh Parsley, Dried Basil Leaves,Salt, Italian Seasoning, Fennel Seeds, and Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles:Store-bought or Homemade Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses:Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg:One egg

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the Ricotta Mixture
  4. Layer the Lasagna
  5. Cover the foil and bake
  6. Let the Lasagna rest before serving
