Grilled Cheese

Description

For a quick easy meal with few ingredients, a grilled cheese is an amazing choice! All you will need is bread, sliced cheese, and butter!

Ingredients

  • Bread: Recipe uses plain white sandwich bread, but sourdough, brioche, or even ciabatta works
  • Cheese: Sliced Cheddar, Sharp Cheddar, Mild Chedder, Gruyere, Brie, any cheese will work!
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
